ABOUT US

Welcome to PUP-T CS Election Express, a web-based voting platform created by Group 6 of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Taguig. This system is specifically designed to revolutionize the electoral process for the Computer Society (CS) organization.


Our mission is to provide a secure, user-friendly, and transparent platform that ensures every vote is counted accurately and securely. The PUP-T CS Election Express is tailored to meet the needs of Computer Society members, prioritizing ease of use, confidence, and fairness in every election.
